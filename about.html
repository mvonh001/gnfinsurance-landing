Independent guidance, Miami-Dade roots

Michael von Heesen is an independent licensed insurance agent based in Bay Harbor Islands, Florida, serving clients throughout all of Miami-Dade County. For more than 10 years, he has helped people work through the parts of healthcare that are hardest to untangle on your own — prescription costs, plan networks, and the decisions that come with turning 65 or reviewing coverage during an enrollment period.

As an independent agent, Michael isn't limited to a single insurance company's products. That independence is what makes it possible to sit down with your actual doctors and your actual medications — not a generic plan summary — and work through the specifics with you, in plain language.

The philosophy behind every review is simple: doctors and medications first. Before any plan comparison starts, Michael checks whether the doctors you see and the medications you take are handled the way you'd expect, as of today's information — then walks you through what that means for your options.
